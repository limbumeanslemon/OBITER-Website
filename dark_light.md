# Theme Toggle

**Current mode: `dark`**

A toggle button (sun/moon icon) sits in the navbar — click it to switch with a curtain animation.
To switch the *default*, tell Claude: "switch to light mode" or "switch to dark mode".
Claude will update `data-theme` in `index.html` and this file.

## What each mode changes

| Section | Dark | Light |
|---|---|---|
| Hero + Now Playing card | Dark navy (always) | Dark navy (always) |
| About strip | Dark navy gradient | Light blue gradient |
| Featured / cs-card | Dark charcoal | Light grey-white |
| Episode cards | Dark navy | White |
| Guest cards | Dark navy | White |
| Team cards | Dark navy | White |
| Dot grid sections | Dark dots | Blue dots |
| Footer | Dark | Dark |
